DCU Timetable

Sick of having to trudge through the DCU website or Moodle to find your timetable when you're on the move? So were we, so we fixed it.

Actively developed by a group of CA students, DCU Timetable is an Android™ app for finding your online class timetable when you need it, all from your mobile phone.

Get the App

Although the app is still in the very early stages of development, it's functional right now at a basic level. You can download it straight to your phone and retrieve your timetable quickly and easily over the web, once you have some form of network connection.

Because it's going through so many rapid changes right now, we're holding off putting it onto the Android Market until it becomes a bit more feature-rich and stable. In the meantime, the latest APK for you to install manually will always be avaliable here (or scan the QR code to your left).

Help Develop It!

We're developing DCU Timetable as an open source project on GitHub™, so anyone is more than welcome to come check out the code, fork it and mess with it in the hopes of something useful eventually happening. In fact, that's encouraged.

Even if you can't help with the code, but have noticed something not acting exactly how it should, or how you think it should, you can help us by filing a bug report. Have an idea for a new feature? Email it to us! Any input will be useful, no matter what it is.
